Payment Security & Processing

At RedTrex Technologies, customer payment security is a top priority. All payments on our website are processed through WebXPay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway trusted by banks and businesses across Sri Lanka.

How Your Payment Is Handled

Payments are processed directly on WebXPay's secure servers
RedTrex never stores your card details or sensitive financial information
All transactions are encrypted and protected using industry-standard security protocols
WebXPay supports multiple trusted cross border payment methods, ensuring reliability and transparency
This approach ensures your payment data remains safe and isolated from our website infrastructure at all times.

Why We Don't Use a Traditional Shopping Cart

Unlike physical goods, the products we offer are digital software licenses that require individual verification, validation, and vendor-side checks before delivery.

To Maintain Accuracy & Prevent Misuse

Each order is processed individually
Payments are tied to a unique order reference
Licenses are issued only after successful payment confirmation

This Method Allows Us To

Prevent duplicate or invalid license issues
Reduce automated abuse and fraudulent transactions
Provide better post-purchase support and tracking
Rather than a mass checkout cart, this controlled flow ensures higher reliability and better customer protection.

Software License Sourcing & Verification

At RedTrex Technologies, all software licenses are sourced through authorized distributors, verified resellers, and trusted enterprise channels. Every license undergoes validation before being delivered to ensure authenticity and usability.

How We Source Licenses

Procured from verified supplier networks and enterprise resellers
Includes surplus, unused, or volume-issued licenses where applicable
Suppliers are reviewed for compliance and reliability

Verification Before Delivery

Each license key is checked for activation validity
Keys are verified as unused and non-duplicated
Delivery is performed only after successful payment confirmation
This verification process ensures customers receive genuine, functional software licenses backed by post-purchase support.

Customer Data & Payment Refunds

At RedTrex Technologies, shopping is simple — no login or account creation is required. We only store the necessary information for order delivery and verification, including your Order details, Name, Phone Number, Email, and Address. All customer data is securely stored on our local servers and will never be shared with third parties.

Data Management

You can request deletion of your information at any time
Deletion requests are usually processed within 1 business hour, but may take up to 1–3 business days
We only retain data necessary for order fulfillment and support

Payment Handling

All payments are securely handled by the payment platform you choose
For fiat payments: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc., handled by WebXPay.com
For crypto payments: platforms like Binance or Bybit handle all transactions; no sensitive crypto credentials are stored on RedTrex servers

Refunds

Refunds are processed only after order verification and confirmation
Fiat refunds via WebXPay are returned to the original payment method, typically within 1–5 business days
Crypto refunds via platforms like Binance or Bybit are returned to the same wallet/account used for payment, subject to network confirmations
Customers receive email notifications once refunds are completed
For any payment-related inquiries, please contact the respective platform’s support: WebXPay Support | Binance Support | Bybit Support
